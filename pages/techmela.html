Tech Mela

TechMela, a vibrant event organized by ISTE in 2023, was designed especially for the fresher batch to give them a unique, outdoor engineering experience beyond the classroom. The event featured an exhibition, a tech quiz, Tech Adda, gaming setups, and fun activities, offering a mix of learning and enjoyment.

Exhibition :

The exhibition showcased innovative projects, including ISTE AEC's creations like ExCV, RC planes, and drones. Attendees got a closer look at project methodologies, tools, and instruments, gaining valuable hands-on insights.

Tech Quiz :

The tech quiz adopted a hybrid format, with participants present at the venue while the questions were displayed virtually through Google Meet. This approach combined face-to-face interaction with online convenience, making the quiz engaging and inclusive.

Tech Adda :

Tech Adda, a collaborative event by ISTE and the Orators Club, was an interactive debate on various technological topics. The event encouraged participants to share ideas, challenge views, and explore the evolving tech world in a lively atmosphere.

The event ended on a fun note, with participants and organizers enjoying games and light-hearted activities. This informal conclusion fostered connections and created lasting memories for everyone involved.
